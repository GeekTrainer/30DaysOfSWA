# Roadmap

Welcome to **#30DaysOfSWA** - a fun way to jumpstart your learning journey into Azure Static Web Apps. Over the next four weeks, we'll learn core concepts, see usage examples, explore developer tools, and discuss best practices to design richer end-to-end user experiences.

We've put together a tenative plan for what we hope to cover - and hope to adapt the content as needed to suit your feedback or interests. So do give us your feedback!
 * Have comments or questions? Post them to the the [Discussions](https://github.com/orgs/staticwebdev/discussions) forum. 
 * Sharing your learnings online? Tweet with `#30DaysOfSWA` so we see it.
